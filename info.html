Shrödinger's Advent calendar, open one box every day!

Learn about quantum physics while you wait for Santa :D

The Shrödinger's Advent calendar was created as a part of the Quantum Games course in Aalto University. We had to make a game or art project that was inspired by quantum physics. I made an Advent calendar where Schrödinger's cat (either dead or alive) tells you fun facts about quantum physics.
